Sales Dashboard in Excel

For this project, I worked with a dataset sourced from Kaggle, which included Walmart sales data from three distinct branches. To develop the ultimate Dashboard, the dataset required initial data cleaning. Subsequently, I delved into data exploration by utilizing fundamental summary statistics and pivot tables. The resulting Dashboard comprises visuals depicting total sales trends over time, sales breakdown by product category, and average customer satisfaction categorized by city and customer type. The graphical insights are customizable using slicers for enhanced filtering capabilities
